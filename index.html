About Us

The African Maths Initiatives is a a collective of mathematicians and mathematics educators working to create a stronger mathematical community and culture of mathematics across Africa.

Description:

AMI now includes mathematicians and mathematics educators around the world who are working to create a stronger mathematical community and culture of mathematics across Africa. By creating a broader base of locally-produced mathematically literate people, we are working to provide Africans with the skills to take on major problems facing the nation from AIDS to climate change.

We are working to create stronger ties between African mathematical communities and the international community in order to raise local standards and improve local conditions. Our vision cuts across all levels of mathematics, from primary schools to universities.
