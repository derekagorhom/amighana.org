African Maths Initiative

On Tuesday, 9th August 2022, the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences Ghanain collaboration with the Ghana Academy of Arts and Sciences and the Ministry of Education, Ghana organised the Francis Allotey Public Lecture to climax the 10th anniversary celebration of the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences Ghana The event was organised to honour the late Professor Francis K.A. Allotey, the founder of the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences Ghana and also one of the founders of the Allotey Maths Camp. The event was held at the auditorium of the Ghana Academy of Arts and Sciences based on the theme, "Developing versatile and innovative STEM leaders for Africa’s scientific excellence and socio-economic development”.

The main speakers for the events were Prof. Neil Toruk (Founder, AIMS NEI) and Prof. Joceylyn Bell-Burnell – Chancellor, University of Dundee (Nobel Prize award winner in Physics in 1974). The Minister of Education, Hon. John Ntim Fordjour, Deputy Minister for Education/Member of Parliament for Assin South Constituency delivered the keynote address...

Allotey Maths Camp

The Allotey Maths Camp which is one of the programs under the African Maths Initiative Ghana (AMI Ghana) program is helping students to identify their strengths in mathematics by exploring various problem-solving skills through mathematical thinking, activities and games.
